Welcome to Anime World

Your favorite anime awaits!

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997.

Haikyu!!

Haikyu!! is a Japanese manga series written by Haruichi Furudate. It was serialized from February 2012 to July 2020, with 45 volumes.

Blue Lock

Blue Lock is a Japanese manga series written by Muneyuki Kaneshiro. It has been serialized in Kodansha's Weekly Shōnen Magazine since August 2018.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a manga series by Koyoharu Gotouge. Serialized from February 2016 to May 2020, with 23 volumes.

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel by Chugong. Serialized since July 2016 and published under D&C Media's Papyrus label.

Bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki never asked for the ability to see ghosts. When his family is attacked by a Hollow, he becomes a Soul Reaper.

Dandadan

Dandadan is a Japanese manga series written by Yukinobu Tatsu. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Shōnen Jump+ since April 2021.

Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online is a Japanese light novel series by Reki Kawahara. It focuses on protagonists Kazuto "Kirito" Kirigaya and Asuna.

Silent Voice

A grade-school student with a hearing impairment is bullied and later seeks to make amends.

Steins Gate

Rintaro Okabe discovers a method of time travel through which they can send text messages to the past.

Mashle: Magic and Muscle

Mash Burnedead is a student at Easton Magic Academy who lacks magic but possesses incredible strength.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Frieren is a mage who has lived for hundreds of years. She reflects on her journey with her companions.

Baccano!

Baccano! is a 2007 anime series adapted from the light novels written by Ryohgo Narita.
